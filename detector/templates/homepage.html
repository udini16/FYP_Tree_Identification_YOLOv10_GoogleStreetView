Identify Malaysian Street Trees with AI

This project uses a YOLOv10 object detection model to identify common trees found along the streets of Malaysia. By leveraging Google Street View, we can automatically scan and classify trees to better understand our urban canopy.

Identifiable Tree Types

Angsana

Also known as 'Sena', this large, deciduous tree is famous for its dense, shade-giving canopy and the "golden showers" of yellow flowers it produces.

Coconut Palm

The iconic 'Pokok Kelapa'. A tall, slender palm found all over Malaysia, vital to both the landscape and cuisine. Easily recognized by its fruit clusters.

Rain Tree

Known as 'Pokok Hujan-Hujan', this tree has a massive, umbrella-shaped canopy that is often wider than it is tall, providing immense shade.

Royal Palm

A tall, majestic palm with a very smooth, concrete-like grey trunk and a bright green crownshaft. Used for formal, grand landscaping.
